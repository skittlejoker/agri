About AgriMarket

Connecting Local Farmers with Conscious Consumers

E-Commerce for Agriculture: Building an Online Marketplace for Local Farmers is an innovative platform designed to connect local farmers directly with consumers, retailers, and distributors through a convenient and transparent online marketplace.

The system aims to empower farmers by providing them with access to a wider market, fair pricing, and real-time sales opportunities, while offering consumers fresh, high-quality agricultural products directly from the source.

This project promotes sustainable agriculture, supports local economies, and integrates digital technology into the agricultural supply chain. Through this platform, farmers can easily list their products, track sales, and communicate with buyers, while customers can browse, order, and pay securely‚Äîall in one place.

Key Features

  • User-friendly marketplace for agricultural products
  • Farmer and buyer registration and profile management
  • Secure online transactions and payment integration
  • Real-time product updates and inventory management
  • Delivery and logistics tracking system
  • Review and feedback system to ensure trust and quality
